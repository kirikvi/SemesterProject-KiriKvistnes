Night in the Museum

Get your family or friends together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays.

Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.

The event starts with a guided tour at 18.00. After the tour, it is covered for a delicious meal in a dining hall reserved for the participants. After the meal there will be a quiz.

For those who dare, there will be an extra tour in our mysterious and scary exhibitions at midnight.

The breakfast buffet is open from 7.00-9.30.
